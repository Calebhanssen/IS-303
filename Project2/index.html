What is Human Trafficking?

Utah's Numbers

Human trafficking is a form of modern slavery that occurs in every state, including Utah.

Missing People in Utah

How Can I Help?

There are many organizations that are built to combat human trafficking. One of which is Operation Underground Railroad which currently supports operation and aftercare efforts in 28 countries and 48 U.S. States.

One way you can help is by donating to the O.U.R. organization which can be done on their website.
